Why are you reading on the steet?

Honestly, fair question, let us explain.

We are two comedians and lately we've been panicking about not knowing enough to be funny. How much do you need to know to be funny? If not book stuff, probs at least a bit of experience stuff. But you can't just sit down and get experience. Can you? Either way, we noticed we don't take any time to actually read up on anything. Instead we spend a lot of time distracted and feeling bad.

We thought, "let's stop feeling bad and DO something". So we got a busking permit and sat out in public. We thought it would make us accountable to catch up on our reading, but also we thought that this feeling of having to take drastic measures to just read something you pick for yourself might be relatable. Maybe our dumb idea might even make someone walking by smile, thinking about how silly it is and if they also feel bad about this same thing, maybe they won't feel so lonely too.

Thanks for stopping by. We know it doesn't really work this way, but you're allowed to read through us if you like. We're reading, so you can relax. Or use seeing this as an excuse to crack open a cold one (a book). Surely if we all are doing it this public reading performance gets less embarassing for us. Though to be fair, our embarassment isn't really your problem. You're free as a bird.
